# Nomad Regulatory-Safe Pitch Narrative

## Positioning Statement
Nomad is a behavioral intelligence layer built on top of open banking data.
We do not hold funds.
We do not execute transactions.
We operate strictly in read-only mode.

## Market Framing
Legacy tools track spending.
Nomad changes spending behavior.

## Problem Statement
Users do not fail from lack of transaction visibility.
Users fail from repeated impulsive behavior.

## Product Claim
Nomad quantifies hidden habit cost and introduces delay-based discipline using observed transaction history.

## Core Proof Point
The average user leaks meaningful annual value through repeat impulse patterns.
Nomad identifies those patterns early and shows projected yearly loss before habits compound.

## Why This Model Scales
- No custody model
- Lower regulatory burden than money-moving products
- Faster launch in new markets where open banking data access exists
- Cleaner AI training signal from behavior-focused datasets

## Compliance-Safe Language (Use in decks and demos)
Use:
- "Read-only financial intelligence"
- "Behavioral analytics on consented transaction data"
- "No transaction execution"
- "No custody"

Avoid:
- "We manage customer money"
- "We control transfers"
- "We hold escrow funds"

## Investor One-Liner
Nomad is the behavioral operating layer for personal finance: it helps users delay impulses and reduce recurring habit loss without touching their money.
