Your record here is permanent. It cannot be deleted. It can only be built — or damaged. Lenders, loan apps, and partner platforms read this record. Not your bank balance. Your behavior.

Part 01

The Lock

Every deposit enters a time-lock immediately. The lock is not punishment. It is the mechanism that separates you from your impulse. Lock durations are calculated as a percentage of your declared monthly income — making this standard universal across all income levels.

Deposit as % of monthly incomeLock Duration
Up to 2%1 day
2% – 5%3 days
5% – 10%7 days
10% – 20%14 days
20% – 40%30 days
40% – 80%60 days
80% – 150%90 days
150%+120 days
The Auto-Relock Rule: Matured funds not touched within 6 hours re-lock automatically at half the original duration. You receive three notifications before this happens. The system defaults to preservation — you must actively choose to withdraw.
The Closed Loop: Withdrawals return only to your verified linked bank account. No peer transfers. No third-party destinations. Your money enters Nomad from one account and exits to the same account. This is non-negotiable.

Part 02

Early Unlock

Early unlock is permitted. Your money is always yours. But every breach is logged permanently and the cost scales with repetition. The system does not trap you. It records what you actually did.

SituationFeeRecord Impact
First early unlock in any 90-day window3% of amount−10 points. Logged with date.
Second early unlock in same window5% of amount−25 points. Loan access suspended 60 days.
Third early unlock in same window7% of amount−50 points. Breach flag visible to partners 180 days.
Breach flags do not disappear. After 180 days of clean behavior they are marked resolved — but the record of the breach remains. A lender sees the full history: the breach, the date, the resolution. This is accurate. This is the point.
