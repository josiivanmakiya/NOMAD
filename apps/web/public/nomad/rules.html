"As people moved beyond where anyone knew them personally, there needed to be a system that recorded how they actually behaved with money. Not their wealth. Their behavior."

— Lewis Tappan, Mercantile Agency, 1841. The idea that built American credit.

Nomad is that system for you. Your record here is permanent. It cannot be deleted. It can only be built — or damaged. Lenders, loan apps, and partner platforms read this record. Not your bank balance. Your behavior.

Part 01

The Lock

Every deposit enters a time-lock immediately. The lock is not punishment. It is the mechanism that separates you from your impulse. Lock durations are set as a percentage of your monthly income — making the standard equal regardless of how much you earn.

Income-relative, not amount-fixed

₦50,000 to someone earning ₦100k/month is a different decision than the same amount to someone earning ₦2M/month. The discipline standard is equal. The naira amount is not the measure — the proportion is.

Auto-Relock: Matured funds not touched within 6 hours re-lock automatically at half the original duration. Three notifications are sent first. The system defaults to preservation — you must actively choose to withdraw.
Closed Loop: Withdrawals return only to your verified linked bank account. No peer transfers. No third-party destinations. Your money enters Nomad from one account and exits to the same account. This is non-negotiable.

Part 02

Early Unlock

Early unlock is permitted. Your money is always yours. But every breach is logged permanently and the cost scales with repetition. The system does not trap you. It records what you actually did.

SituationFeeRecord Impact
First early unlock in any 90-day window3% of amount−10 points. Logged with date.
Second early unlock in same 90-day window5% of amount−25 points. Protocol Breach. Loan access suspended 60 days.
Third early unlock in same 90-day window7% of amount−50 points. Breach flag visible to lender partners for 180 days.
Breach flags do not disappear. After 180 days of clean behavior they are marked resolved — but the record remains. A lender sees the full history: the breach, the date, the resolution. This is accurate. This is the point.

Part 03

Discipline Score

Every action earns or costs points. Streaks are rewarded exponentially — because consistency over time is the entire signal lenders need.

ActionPointsWhy
Deposit locked+1Record begins.
Lock matures without breach+5Core behavior. Worth 5× the deposit action.

First early unlock (90-day window)−10Single breach. Recoverable.
Protocol Breach (2nd unlock)−25Pattern signal. Loan access paused.
Chronic Breach (3rd unlock)−50Behavioral flag. Lender-visible for 180 days.
The streak logic: A 30-day streak (+15) equals 3 completed locks. A 90-day streak (+50) equals 10 locks. A 180-day streak (+120) equals 24 locks. The longer your run, the more valuable each clean day becomes. Losing a 170-day streak costs far more than losing a 10-day streak.

Part 05

Failure States

A failure state is not just a point deduction. It changes what the system shows to lenders. Failure is permitted — it is recorded accurately.

Level 1

Single Breach

  • First early unlock in 90-day window
  • −10 points deducted
  • Logged with date and amount
  • Recoverable within same period

Level 2

Pattern Breach

  • Second early unlock in same 90-day window
  • −25 points deducted
  • Protocol Breach flag triggered
  • Loan access suspended 60 days regardless of score

Level 3

Chronic Breach

  • Third early unlock in same 90-day window
  • −50 points deducted
  • Breach flag visible to all lender partners
  • Flag active for 180 days of clean behavior
  • Cannot advance conduct levels during flag period

Level 4

Dormancy

  • Account inactive 90+ days with locked funds
  • 3 notifications over 7-day window
  • No response triggers safety auto-relock
  • Dormancy period noted on record
Breach flags resolve — they do not disappear. After the 180-day clean period the flag is marked resolved. The original breach date, amount, and context remain permanently on file. A lender reading your record in year three sees year one clearly. This is not punitive — it is accurate.

Part 06

The Withdrawal Rule

You can always withdraw matured funds to your verified bank account. This is non-negotiable. Your money is yours. What changes is your record.

Closed Loop. Always.

Withdrawals return to your verified linked account only. No peer transfers. No third-party destinations. The narrow transfer surface protects your capital from social pressure — no one can ask you to send money from your Nomad balance directly.
